PROFILE


Trading over 40 years The Garage Dunboyne is a family run locally owned business. We specialise in all types of mechanical repairs with no job too big or small. We have a dedicated team of fully trained professionals who can cater for all your automotive needs.

We source tyres from Hankook in order to guarantee their quality.Your tyres are the only part of the car which are in contact with the road, the quality of tyres therefore has a big impact on the drivability of vehicles. We stock all sizes available on the market from Hankook.

AIR CON

 
At the Garage Dunboyne a full air con service is now available in both new and old cars we De-gas & deep vacuum to remove all old refrigerant. We also do a System pressure test and Replace old compressor oil. Leaving you with a perfectly serviced air con system.

Tyre Sales

We stock a large range of tyres to cater for all sizes. We can source any tyre requested.Brands include: Hankook, Michelin, Pirelli, Bridgestone, Vredestein and Continental.We have the tyre to suit any budget!

Diagnostics

We have the latest in diagnostic equipment to diagnose most vehicle models running faults. Having the latest diagnostic equipment to hand helps us find faults quicker. If a fault is found we can usually deal with it the next day ensuring labour costs are kept to a minimum.

Wheel Alignment

Our new Laser Wheel Alignment system takes wheel alignment equipment to the next generation. This technology provides the ultimate performance in fast, easy and accurate align-ments while minimizing the possibilities of errors due to operator and vehicle variables.

Batteries

We stock a large selection of batteries for all makes and models. In the unlikely event that the battery you require is out of stock, we would normally be able to supply you with one within the hour.

Vehicle Repairs

We specialise in all types of vehicle repairs including timing belts, clutches, engine rebuilding, panel beating and Pre and Post NCT repairs.

Car Accesories
